Writer / Author Program

Apply to write

My Novel World is open to any reader who wants to publish their own novels here. Every application is reviewed individually — we're not looking for anything in particular, just a genuine sense that you're ready to write. Once approved, you're fully independent: your own books, your own publishing schedule, your own Write dashboard.

Shown publicly everywhere instead of your account username — on book covers, chapter pages, and your author profile.
